WRIGHT PARK

Wright Park is one of Baguio’s most popular attractions and for a good reason! This park is home to beautiful gardens, interesting historical landmarks, and a variety of fun activities. If you’re looking for things to do in Baguio City, Wright Park should definitely be at the top of your list!

CAMP JOHN HAY

Camp John Hay, a haven tucked away in the City of Pines, offers you a taste of joy and adventure. Each and every corner is jam-packed with entertainment options, activity hubs, retail stores, and dining establishments.

Learn More

BOTANICAL GARDEN

If you’re looking for a picturesque and relaxing outdoor escape, look no further than the Botanical Garden in Baguio City. Nestled in the heart of the Philippines’ Cordillera region, this sprawling garden is home to various indigenous plants and flowers.

MINES VIEW PARK

Mines View Park is one of the famous tourist spots across Baguio pack with shops selling snacks, trinkets, botanical items, and others. Picture taking services with traditional Igorot clothes, horses, dogs and more are also available towards the lower half of the park.
